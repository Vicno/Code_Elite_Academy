Why to learn Backend Development


Backend Development is everything that happens behind the curtains. Where does the data go? What makes the website run? How does it know what I was doing on the last page? All that and more is the work the Backend is responsible for handling. You can see it as the clockwork of a clock, without it, nothing works. If what you fancy is getting into the thick and rough of the code, analizing data and handling the logic of the page, then Backend is for you.
